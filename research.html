/research


/current

I am currently working with Trilinos a large set of packages designed for solving different engineering and scientific problems. My work focuses on global unknown numbering of meshes in a parallel environment, specifically for the purpose of solving multiphysics simulations. This work is done in the currently unreleased Panzer package.

A presentation I gave at the conclusion of summer 2012 outlining some of the work I have done in this area can be found here.

I am currently continuing this work during the 2012-2013 academic year as part of my thesis project.

/past

Before working in global numbering, I spent time working on several parallel environments, primarily CUDA. This work was overseen by my advisor, Dr. Michael Heroux.
