/2012-08-03

Vim hint: nnoremap K i<CR><Esc>k$

This remaps K to insert break at the cursor position and put in a new line. This makes it the more reasonable pair of J, which joins lines. The default behavior of K is to look up the man page of the word under the cursor. In my opinion this functionality is only really important if you're doing bash scripting. I don't use it enough to justify its high profile position.
